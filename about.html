About

Simple appointment reminders for small businesses.

Appointment Reminder helps solo operators and small service businesses send clear, professional reminders without paying for a large scheduling platform they may not need.

Focused on one job

Send reminders quickly without a booking system, CRM, or complicated setup.

Made for service businesses

Works well for barbers, trainers, tutors, therapists, cleaners, and other appointment-based businesses.

Faster than manual follow-up

Fill in the details once, review the message, and send it from the channel that fits best.

Built to stay lightweight

The goal is fewer missed appointments, not feature overload.

Why it exists

Some businesses already know how they book clients. What they need is a simpler way to follow up and reduce no-shows.

  • Reduce missed appointments and last-minute confusion
  • Send more polished reminders without extra admin work
  • Keep the workflow approachable for non-technical users

What comes next

Right now, the product is focused on reminders. If customers later want more advanced tools like saved client records or scheduling support, those features may be added carefully as the product grows.

Keep it simple:

Use the reminder tool when you want a fast, professional follow-up process without the weight of a full scheduling platform. Go back to the reminder tool.
