Privacy Policy

This tool processes client contact information for the purpose of preparing and sending appointment reminders.

Information Used

The information entered into the form may include phone numbers, email addresses, names, addresses, appointment details, and other reminder content.

How Information Is Used

Information is used only to generate and send appointment reminders and related calendar details through selected delivery providers.

Third-Party Providers

Messages may be delivered through third-party providers such as Brevo and, in the future, Twilio. These providers may process contact information as part of message delivery.

Your Responsibility

You should only enter contact information for clients you are authorized to contact and should avoid entering unnecessary sensitive information.

Policy Updates

If you plan to use this tool commercially, this policy should be reviewed and tailored to your business practices before launch.
